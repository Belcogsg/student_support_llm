Course Registration
Students must register all courses within the first two weeks of every semester using the UDSM ARIS system https://aris3.udsm.ac.tz. Late registration can attract penalties.

Accommodation
Students apply for accommodation through the ARIS portal. Allocation depends on availability.

Fees
Tuition fees must be paid before course registration can be completed. This is done by obtaining a control number on ARIS then paying through various payment methods.

Academic Calendar
The academic calendar is published before every academic year on the university website https://www.udsm.ac.tz. The almanac can also be found there.

ICT Support
Students experiencing login problems should contact the ICT Directorate.

Library services
Library services are available from Monday to Saturday, 8:00am to 5:30pm. Students can borrow books using their valid university identity card.

Examinations
Students must possess a valid ID before sitting for examinations. Unauthorized materials like phones, smartwatches, analog watches, airpods, meta glasses etc are strictly prohibited in the exam room and anyone found with them will be heavily penalized.

Graduation
Students qualify for graduation after completing all programme requirements.
