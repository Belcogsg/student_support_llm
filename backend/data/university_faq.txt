Course Registration
Students must register all courses within the first two weeks of every semester using the ARIS system.

Accommodation
Students apply for accommodation through the ARIS portal. Allocation depends on availability.

Fees
Tuition fees must be paid before course registration can be completed.

Academic Calendar
The academic calendar is published before every academic year on the university website.

ICT Support
Students experiencing login problems should contact the ICT Directorate.

Library
Students can borrow books using their university identity card.

Examinations
Students must possess a valid examination permit before sitting for examinations.

Graduation
Students qualify for graduation after completing all programme requirements.
